Getting Started
  1. Go to polly-connect.com and click Register to create your account
  2. Plug in Polly and connect to the "Polly-Setup" WiFi network on your phone
  3. Enter the 6-digit claim code above along with your home WiFi credentials, then hit Connect
  4. On the website, go to Settings → Devices and enter the same claim code
  5. Done! Polly is yours — start sharing stories and memories
