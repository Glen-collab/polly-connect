  • View and listen to stories
  • Browse family photos and record photo stories
  • See the family tree and add family members
  • Send and check messages
  • Verify transcriptions (fix what Polly heard)
  • View the legacy book
  • Record a memory from your phone

Remember Where Things Are

Your personal parrot that remembers everything.

Store: "My keys are on the hook by the door"

Find: "Where are my keys?"

Lost: "I can't find the hammer" — Polly responds like Mom

Found: "I found it" — Polly asks where it was

Web: Add items in bulk on the Stored Items page

Daily Reminders

Never miss a vitamin, pill, or appointment.

Setup: Add reminders on the Care page with times and days

Polly says: "It's 4 AM, time to take your vitamin D, fish oil, and creatine."

Repeat: "Repeat that" if you missed it

Family Message Board

Leave a message. Polly nags until they check it.

Leave: "Tell mom I'll be home late"

Check: "Check messages"

Clear: "Clear messages"

Nag: Polly squawks "Message!" every 15 min until checked

Web: Type messages on the Messages page too

Hear Your Father's Blessing Forever

Record a blessing once. Play it back any time in their real voice.

Record: Go to Prayers page → Record a Blessing

Play: "Play a meal blessing" or "Play Dad's bedtime blessing"

Categories: meal, bedtime, morning, gratitude, general

AI Prayers

Polly leads a prayer tailored to how you're feeling.

General: "Say a prayer"

For someone: "Pray for Nolan" or "Pray for world peace"

Mood: "I'm having a hard day" or "I feel alone"

15 categories: hope, anxiety, grief, healing, faith, gratitude, and more

Fun Stuff

Polly's got personality.

Jokes: "Tell me a joke" or "Tell me a kid joke"

Weather: "What's the weather?"

Bible: "Read me a bible verse" or "Read me a Psalm"

Time: "What time is it?" or "What's the date?"

Bird sounds: Polly squawks and chatters on her own schedule

Polly's Sounds & Sleep

Control when Polly chirps and when she sleeps.

Volume: Settings → Bird Sound Volume, Voice Volume, Blessing Volume

Frequency: Settings → Short Squawks and Long Chatter intervals (or Off)

Quiet hours: Settings → Bedtime and Wake up times

Snooze: Settings → Snooze Polly for 30 min to 8 hours

Voice: "Be quiet" to stop a squawk mid-play
